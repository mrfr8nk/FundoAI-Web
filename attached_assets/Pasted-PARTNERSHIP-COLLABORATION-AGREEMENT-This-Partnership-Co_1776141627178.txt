Collectively referred to as the “Parties.” 
1. PURPOSE OF THE AGREEMENT 
The purpose of this Agreement is to establish the terms and conditions under which the Partner 
will collaborate with the Founder to support, market, and financially sponsor the FUNDO AI 
project (“Project”), ensuring smooth operations, growth, and sustainability. 
2. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES 
2.1 Founder Responsibilities 
The Founder shall: 
• Maintain ownership and leadership of the FUNDO AI project. 
• Manage development, operations, and technical direction of the project. 
• Provide updates and progress reports regarding project status. 
• Ensure responsible use of funds for project growth. 
2.2 Partner Responsibilities 
The Partner shall: 
• Provide financial support as agreed to support the project’s growth. 
• Assist in marketing, outreach, and promotional activities. 
• Support strategic planning and growth initiatives. 
• Promote the project responsibly and professionally. 
3. FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENT & OWNERSHIP SHARE 
• The Partner shall receive 40% ownership (equity share) in the FUNDO AI project in 
recognition of financial sponsorship, marketing contribution, and strategic support. 
• The Founder retains 60% ownership and remains the primary owner of the project. 
• The Partner’s 40% share applies to profits, revenues, and valuation derived from the 
project unless otherwise agreed in writing. 
• Any financial contributions made by the Partner shall be documented and used for 
operational, marketing, and development expenses related to the project. 
• Both Parties agree that future adjustments to ownership percentages require 
mutual written agreement. 
4. OWNERSHIP & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY 
• All intellectual property, branding, code, designs, and materials related to FUNDO AI 
remain the property of the Founder unless otherwise agreed in writing. 
• The Partner shall not claim ownership of intellectual property beyond the agreed 
equity share unless explicitly agreed in writing. 
5. CONFIDENTIALITY 
Both Parties agree to: 
• Keep all confidential information private and secure. 
• Not disclose project information, strategies, or trade secrets to third parties without 
written consent. 
6. DECISION-MAKING 
• The Founder retains final decision-making authority regarding technical direction, 
branding, and project roadmap. 
• The Partner may contribute ideas and recommendations, which will be considered 
in good faith. 
7. DURATION & TERMINATION 
• This Agreement shall remain in effect until terminated by either Party. 
• Either Party may terminate this Agreement with written notice. 
• Upon termination, confidential information and assets must remain protected. 
8. AMENDMENTS 
Any modifications to this Agreement must be made in writing and agreed upon by both Parties.
